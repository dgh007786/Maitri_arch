Horizon multi-specialty hospital

  • Location          : Bhavnagar
  • Area               : 2500sqft
  • Status             : Completed
  • Client's name : Arvindbhai

     Hospital is designed while keeping its function in mind. General wards for both men and women are done separately using olive green shade walls with ink art done on it to ensure calm n peaceful environment.
     Gynae labour room has been designed with a concept of nature inspired ceiling using artificial lightings and 3d art work that keeps one attracted towards it. To ensure speedy healing of the patient vibrant shades and biophilic elements are used such as creeper in ceiling design.
